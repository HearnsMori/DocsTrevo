Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common queries

What is Trevo?

Trevo is a collaborative platform that helps individuals and teams manage their projects effectively. It offers various tools such as task tracking, team collaboration, and project visualization.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your sensitive data is secure through encryption. This ensure that your information is safe at all times.

Is Trevo available on mobile devices?

Yes, Trevo is fully responsive and can be accessed from mobile devices, ensuring you can manage your projects on the go.

How do I integrate Trevo with other tools?

Trevo provides various integrations with popular tools like Slack, Google Drive, and Trello. You can connect your accounts directly within the settings panel.

Can I collaborate with my team?

Absolutely! Trevo is designed for teams. You can assign tasks, share files, and communicate directly within the platform to enhance collaboration.
