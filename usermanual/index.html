Trevo User Manual

A comprehensive guide to using Trevo efficiently.

Login and Signup

To get started with Trevo, you need to log in or sign up for an account:

  • Login: Enter your username and password to access your account.
  • Signup: Register with your email address, username, and password. (Password recovery is not yet implemented.)

Home Page

Upon logging in, you'll be redirected to the Home page. Initially, no apps will be displayed, but you can install apps from the in-app store by clicking the plus button.

The Home page is where you manage your apps, and you can add friends or explore other app functionalities.

Adding Friends

You can add friends from the Home page. On the top right of the screen, click the "add friend" icon to search for and send friend requests to other users.

This allows you to connect and interact with others within the app.

Navigation Bar

At the bottom of the Home screen, you'll find the navigation bar with three essential tabs:

  • Home Page: Takes you back to the main page.
  • Message Page: Lets you communicate with the AI to automate actions within your apps.
  • Task Page: Allows you to manage and edit your automated tasks.

Message Page

On the Message page, you can interact with the AI. Key functionalities include:

  • Scheduling: Create tasks and set schedules with different timeframes.
  • App Interaction: Select which app the AI should use to complete tasks.
  • Storage Management: Send data between apps by instructing the AI to manage storage (e.g., transferring files).

Task Page

The Task page allows you to view, edit, and manage your automated tasks. You can configure tasks, update their details, and modify their settings.

Additionally, in the top right of the page, there is a settings option where you can update your account details or add additional email addresses (up to 3).
